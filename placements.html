OUR PLACEMENT POLICY

Here at VSIT we have tie-ups with various software organizations. We also conduct on campus interviews and bootcamps. Our job oriented courses and their curriculum includes interview prepration classes. You will also be guided towards buliding a strong portfolio and resume which can increase the chances of placement. We also conduct regular workshops for personality development and softskills which can provide an edge to you on the day of interview.

Eligibility criteria:

  • In order to be eligible for our placement program one needs to have 90% attendance.
  • Student must obtain atleast 60% marks on our 3 quiz tests that are held after completion of each module.
  • Students must submit all the projects assigned to them on time.
  • They should also clear mock interview rounds which are held on a regular basis in our campus.

Our Job Oriented Programs


Full Stack Development

Full Stack development courses are on boom today in the computer science. Full Stack developers work on both Front end and Back end parts of the Applications. After the course you can start your career as full-stack Developer / front-end / back-end developer or you can also work as a Freelance Coder.

Data Analysis
